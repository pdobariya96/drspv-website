## The Problem

A mid-sized recruitment agency based in Birmingham, UK, with annual turnover of GBP 4.2 million, was struggling with escalating accounting costs. Their in-house finance function comprised:

- A full-time bookkeeper (GBP 28,000/year)
- A part-time management accountant (GBP 22,000/year for 3 days/week)
- An external chartered accountant firm for year-end statutory accounts, corporation tax, and VAT returns (GBP 18,000/year)

**Total annual accounting cost: GBP 68,000**

The company faced additional pain points:

- **Chronic delays** in monthly management reports — the bookkeeper was perpetually 6-8 weeks behind on bank reconciliations
- **VAT filing errors** — HMRC had issued two penalty notices in 18 months for late and incorrect VAT returns under Making Tax Digital (MTD) requirements
- **No real-time financial visibility** — the directors received profit and loss statements only once a quarter, making it difficult to manage cash flow for a business with 180+ temporary workers on weekly payroll
- **Staff turnover risk** — the bookkeeper had indicated plans to retire within 12 months, and replacing her at current UK salary levels was proving difficult

The directors explored UK-based outsourced accounting firms but found that quotes ranged from GBP 48,000 to GBP 60,000 annually — a marginal improvement at best.

## What DRSPV Did

Our CAs designed a comprehensive outsourced accounting solution tailored to UK regulatory requirements:

**Phase 1 — Transition (Weeks 1-4):**
- Conducted a full review of the company's Xero accounting system and existing processes
- Migrated historical data and verified opening balances
- Set up standardised chart of accounts aligned with UK GAAP (FRS 102 Section 1A)
- Configured automated bank feeds and reconciliation rules in Xero
- Established MTD-compliant VAT return processes through Xero's HMRC integration

**Phase 2 — Ongoing Service Delivery:**
Our team in India now handles the following on a weekly and monthly cycle:

| Service | Frequency | UK Equivalent Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Bookkeeping & bank reconciliation | Weekly | Part of bookkeeper role |
| Payroll processing (180+ temps) | Weekly | Typically outsourced at GBP 8-12/employee/month |
| Accounts payable & receivable management | Weekly | Part of bookkeeper role |
| Monthly management accounts (P&L, BS, Cash Flow) | Monthly (by 10th of following month) | Management accountant role |
| VAT return preparation & MTD filing | Quarterly | External accountant |
| Year-end statutory accounts preparation | Annually | External accountant |
| Corporation tax computation support | Annually | External accountant |

**Phase 3 — Value Additions:**
- Introduced a weekly cash flow dashboard accessible via Google Sheets — updated every Monday by 10 AM UK time
- Set up automated aged debtor alerts — reducing average debtor days from 52 to 34
- Created a monthly KPI pack covering gross margin per placement, contractor utilisation, and overhead ratio

## The Result

| Parameter | Before DRSPV | After DRSPV |
|---|---|---|
| **Annual Accounting Cost** | GBP 68,000 | **GBP 27,600** |
| **Cost Saving** | — | **GBP 40,400 (59.4%)** |
| Monthly Reports Delivery | 6-8 weeks delayed | **By 10th of following month** |
| VAT Filing Accuracy | 2 HMRC penalties in 18 months | **Zero penalties in 24 months** |
| Bank Reconciliation Currency | 6-8 weeks behind | **Weekly — reconciled within 3 working days** |
| Debtor Days | 52 days | **34 days** |
| Financial Visibility | Quarterly P&L only | **Weekly cash flow + monthly full accounts** |

**The 60% cost saving was achieved while simultaneously improving:**
- Timeliness — from quarterly to monthly (and weekly for cash flow)
- Accuracy — zero VAT penalties since engagement
- Breadth of service — added payroll, KPI reporting, and cash flow dashboards that the company did not have before

The engagement has been running for over 24 months. The retiring bookkeeper was not replaced, and the part-time management accountant role was discontinued. The company now retains a UK-based chartered accountant only for statutory audit sign-off (GBP 6,000/year), which is included in the GBP 27,600 total.
